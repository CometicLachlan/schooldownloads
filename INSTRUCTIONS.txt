Instructions

Listed below are the instructions to download and install items from this github page.

INSTALLING STREAM DECK SOFTWARE:

1. UNZIP UNIEXTRACT2
2. Go into the UniExtract2 RC3 folder, then the UniExtract2 Folder. Then just launch the app UniExtract 2
3. Download the Stream Deck installer from the school downloads Google Drive.
4. Inside of UniExtract in the first box, tap the three little dots and select the stream deck installer
5. Make sure that the extract method is isMSI Unpacker extraction
6. Go back into the first folder (Downloads or whatever) and then there should be a folder, launch it and the app should be in there
